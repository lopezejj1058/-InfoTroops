  • Edward Lopez Jr

    I'm an Army Veteran that served Post 9/11 as A 91M10 Hospital Food Service Specialist at Ireland Army Hospital. When I completed my tour of duty it was a little difficult to get back into the swing of things and I had no real direction on how or what to look for as far as connections with veterans services and how to get in touch with other veterans on how to adapt to civilian lifestyle. Our team is dedicated to helping veterans of all types to get there goals met in the civilian sector.


  • Samuel Vargas

    I am a Veteran of the Army National Guard. I served from 2006-2014 as a 42F- Human Resources Informaiton Systems Management Specialist. I served in O.I.F. from 08-09 and in South Korea in 2010. My transition from military life to civilian life was fairly easy for me. It wasnt until I was in need of emergency services that I found myself franticly searching the web for available resources. This is the reason I believe Infotroops.us is needed. To help aid veterans with resources that are available to them the second they discharge from duty.
